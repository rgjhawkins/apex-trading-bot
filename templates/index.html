A rolling stop that automatically moves up as price rises, locking in profit. It trails X% below the highest price the trade has ever reached. If price drops back by that %, the trade exits.

Breakeven Stop
After the first take profit target (TP1) is hit, the stop loss automatically moves to your entry price. This makes the remaining position risk-free — worst case you exit at zero gain.
